About Me

What I love to do outside the classroom.

Track & Cross Country

Sub-four minute miler. Division I All-American. Ivy League champion. Track is a huge part of my life — I commit 30+ hours a week to training and competition while maintaining a rigorous course load. I'm also a Princeton University record holder in the 600m. Running has given me lifelong friends and instilled values like discipline, teamwork, and coachability.

The Babb Trophy

Awarded annually to the freshman member of the track team who through sportsmanship, influence, and performance has contributed most to the sport at Princeton.

Photography

I've been into photography ever since my Dad let me borrow his old Nikon D5100. I enjoy shooting natural landscapes, wildlife, and action shots.

Surfing

My Dad is an avid surfer and taught me how to conquer the Jersey Shore waves from a young age. I love to hit the waves whenever possible — even in the dead of winter.
